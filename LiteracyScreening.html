▶ Supplemental Phonological Assessments (PAST & PASI)

Enter results from supplemental phonological awareness assessments for a deeper diagnostic profile. These assessments help pinpoint the specific phonological processing skills that may be driving below-benchmark DIBELS scores.

PAST (Phonological Awareness Screening Test — Kilpatrick, Jan 2024 update)

Enter the Correct and Automatic scores for each developmental level. Mastery requires meeting the minimum on both columns. Levels progress from syllable awareness (D–E) through onset-rime (F–G) to basic and advanced phoneme manipulation (H–M). Total: /52 each column.

PASI (Phonemic Awareness Screener for Intervention — 95% Group)

Enter the number correct for each subtest (out of 5). Mastered = 4–5  |  Emerging = 3  |  Needs Instruction = 0–2. Nine subtests assess: isolation, blending, segmentation, addition, and deletion of phonemes.
